Q: I ordered multiple products or a large quantity, will my order ship together?

Sometimes we split larger orders into multiple shipments. However, we can guarantee that they will ship at the same time. This is more typical with orders of multiple 48 sets of assorted cards.


For any additional questions about shipping, please reach out to us at help@twigspaper.comand we will happily assist you.

Q: Do you ship to PO boxes and FPO/APO/DPO Addresses?

We are happy to offer shipping to PO boxes and FPO/APO/DPO addresses with USPS.
